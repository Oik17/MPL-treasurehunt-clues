Clue 2

I sit near a mind that sparked many a light,
Inventive, resourceful, a genius of flight.
Where machines and motors took their first stand,
A pioneer's legacy across the land.
Yet just a stone's throw from this man of fame,
I offer fuel of a different name.
Not for engines or gears, but for the soul,
Where minds take a break, and bellies find their goal.
What am I, this place of rest,
Near a creator whose work was the best?
