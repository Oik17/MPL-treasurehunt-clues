Clue 2

I shape the future, yet am crafted from the past,
With parts from many, to make one whole at last.
I take what's broken, twisted, or torn,
And give it life anew, as if reborn.
From cells to tissues, I weave and mold,
A living puzzle, intricate and bold.
What am I, this art of life renewed,
Where science and biology fuse?
